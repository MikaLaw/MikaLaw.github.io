Basic app mechanics

  • Create a task and a template based on it. Scheduling will become even easier!

  • Create habits and get better every day. Set goals and achieve them!

  • Distribute tasks and habits into lists. Group activities to emphasize on the really important activities.

  • Schedule notifications and keep track of statistics. A detailed report is available at any time.
